ATM Moving FAQs

How much does it cost to move an ATM?

ATM moving costs typically start at $300 for local moves within the Phoenix metro area. Final pricing depends on the ATM type, weight, distance, floor level, and access conditions. We provide free quotes for all ATM relocation projects.

Do you have insurance for ATM moving?

Yes, we carry full commercial liability insurance covering ATM transport and installation. We can provide certificates of insurance for banks, credit unions, and commercial clients upon request.

What types of ATMs can you move?

We move all types of ATMs including freestanding units, through-the-wall ATMs, lobby ATMs, drive-up ATMs, and ITMs (Interactive Teller Machines). Our equipment handles machines any weight.

Can you relocate ATMs overnight?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling including overnight, weekend, and after-hours ATM relocations to minimize disruption to your business operations.

Do you bolt down ATMs after moving?

Yes, we provide professional ATM anchoring and bolt-down services as part of the installation. Proper anchoring is essential for ATM security and compliance requirements.

How long does an ATM move take?

Most local ATM moves are completed within 2-4 hours, including removal, transport, and installation. Complex installations or multi-ATM projects may require additional time.
