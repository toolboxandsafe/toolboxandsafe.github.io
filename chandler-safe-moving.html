Neighborhood Notes

  • Ocotillo: Lakefront master-planned community. Newer homes, excellent access. Many tech executives live here.
  • Sun Groves: Established family community. Mix of home sizes, usually straightforward moves.
  • Chandler Heights: More rural feel, larger lots. Some properties have longer driveways — good for parking equipment close.
  • Downtown Chandler: Historic district with older homes. Narrower doorways possible — I assess before moving.
  • Circle G: Horse properties and custom homes. Larger layouts, premium safe installations.

The Intel Effect

Intel's Chandler campus employs 12,000+ people. Add PayPal, Wells Fargo, Microchip, and Northrop Grumman, and you have one of Arizona's highest concentrations of high-income professionals.

What does the tech corridor mean for safe moving?

  • Relocations: Tech workers transfer in from California, Texas, and out of state. They often bring existing safes or buy new ones after settling.
  • Premium purchases: Higher incomes = higher-end safes. I move more Liberty Colonial and Fort Knox Defender series in Chandler than almost anywhere else.
  • Document security: Engineers with security clearances take document protection seriously. Fireproof document safes are common in Chandler homes.
  • Watch & jewelry storage: Beyond firearms, Chandler professionals collect watches, jewelry, and valuables that need secure storage.

Chandler-Specific Considerations

Tech Corridor Traffic

The Price Road corridor and areas near Intel can have heavy traffic during shift changes (7-9 AM, 4-6 PM). For moves in West Chandler or near Intel, morning appointments before 7 AM or midday work best.

Newer Construction Standards

Chandler's building boom means many homes have modern layouts with 36" doorways, reinforced garage floors, and open floor plans. This makes safe installation easier than in older cities.

Multi-Story Homes

Chandler has more two-story homes than some East Valley cities. If your safe is going upstairs (or coming down), let me know — that changes the equipment and crew requirements.
