Gilbert-Specific Considerations

HOA Rules

Most Gilbert communities have active HOAs. Some have restrictions on delivery vehicles or working hours. Let me know if your HOA requires advance notice or has specific rules about work trucks.

Garage-Based Living

Gilbert families use their garages — workshops, gyms, storage. If your safe is going in a finished garage space, measure the clearance. I've seen garages with gym equipment that need to be rearranged before I can position the safe.

New Construction

Gilbert is still growing, especially in Southeast Gilbert and toward Queen Creek. For new builds, coordinate safe delivery after your final walkthrough and flooring installation.
