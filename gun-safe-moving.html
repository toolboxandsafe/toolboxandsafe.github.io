Gun Safe Moving FAQs

Gun safe moving costs typically start at $200 for smaller safes and local moves within the Phoenix metro area. Larger safes (800+ lbs) or moves involving stairs typically range from $300-$600. We provide free quotes based on safe weight, distance, and access conditions.

Yes, we have specialized stair-climbing equipment and techniques for moving heavy gun safes up or down stairs. We assess the stairway width, turn radius, and landing space to ensure safe transport. Stair moves typically add $50-$150 depending on the number of stairs and safe weight.

Absolutely. Our equipment handles gun safes up to 2,000 lbs. We've moved large Liberty, Browning, Fort Knox, and custom vault safes throughout Arizona. We use hydraulic dollies and rigging equipment specifically designed for heavy safe transport.

Yes, we recommend removing all firearms, ammunition, and valuables before the move. This reduces weight for safer transport and protects your belongings. We do not transport firearms or ammunition directly - only the safe itself.

Yes, we carry full commercial liability insurance covering all safe transport. Your gun safe is protected during the entire move. We can provide certificates of insurance upon request.

Most local gun safe moves take 1-2 hours from arrival to completion. Complex moves involving stairs, multiple floors, or very heavy safes (1000+ lbs) may take 2-3 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when we provide your quote.

Yes, we offer anchoring services to bolt your safe to a concrete floor. This prevents theft by tipping or removal. Anchoring is recommended for fire-rated safes and adds approximately $50-$100 to the service. We use the bolt holes pre-drilled in your safe.
