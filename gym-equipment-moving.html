Common Questions

How much does it cost to move a treadmill?

Treadmill moving starts at $150 for local moves under 25 miles. Factors that affect price include: stairs (I can do them!), distance, and if the treadmill needs to be tilted to fit through doorways. Call for a free quote.

Do I need to disassemble my home gym?

Not usually. Most home gyms can be moved assembled. If disassembly is needed (narrow hallways, tight corners), I'll handle it and reassemble at the destination. This is included in your quote.

Can you move gym equipment upstairs?

Yes. I have specialized stair-climbing equipment that makes this safe and efficient. There's an additional $75/flight charge for stairs, but it's far better than damaging your equipment or walls trying to muscle it up.

What about the electronic screens on Pelotons?

I take special care with connected fitness equipment. Screens are protected, and I know how to handle these machines without damaging sensors or displays. I've moved dozens of Pelotons without issue.

Do you move commercial gym equipment?

Yes! I work with gyms, fitness centers, and physical therapy offices. Whether you're moving one piece or outfitting a new facility, I can help. Commercial jobs get custom quotes based on the full scope of work.
