Hot Tub Moving in Arizona

Arizona's hot tub moving comes with unique challenges. Block walls without gate access. Pool decks with limited clearance. Desert landscaping that can't support heavy equipment. We've seen it all and have solutions.

Common Arizona Hot Tub Moves:

  • Backyard to backyard: Moving a spa between homes in the Phoenix metro area
  • Over-the-wall lifts: Crane service when there's no side gate access
  • Pool deck placement: Positioning spas next to existing pools
  • Covered patio moves: Getting hot tubs under Arizona room structures
  • New home delivery: Bringing spas from dealers or previous owners
  • Old spa removal: Hauling away broken or unwanted hot tubs

Before Your Hot Tub Move:

  • Drain completely 24-48 hours before moving
  • Disconnect electrical (we recommend a licensed electrician)
  • Remove or secure the cover
  • Clear the path from spa to truck access point
  • Measure gates, doorways, and tight spots - let us know dimensions
  • Have the destination pad or area prepared

Hot Tub Moving FAQ

How much does it cost to move a hot tub?

Hot tub moving typically starts around $350-$600 for local moves in Phoenix, depending on size, weight, and accessibility. Crane lifts for over-fence moves add additional cost. We provide free quotes for all hot tub moves.

Do I need to drain my hot tub before moving?

Yes, your hot tub must be completely drained before we arrive. A full hot tub can weigh 3,000-6,000+ lbs with water. Drain it 24-48 hours before and disconnect electrical.

Can you move a hot tub over a fence or wall?

Yes, we arrange crane service for over-fence lifts. Many Arizona backyards have block walls with no gate - we have solutions for these situations.

How do you protect my hot tub during the move?

We use professional spa dollies, furniture blankets, and straps designed for hot tub transport. Shells, control panels, and jets are all protected. Your spa is fully insured.

Do you reconnect electrical after moving?

We position your hot tub exactly where you want it. For electrical hookup (most require 220V), we recommend a licensed electrician and can refer spa-experienced electricians.

Can you remove an old hot tub for disposal?

Yes, we offer hot tub removal and haul-away. Let us know if you need removal only, or removal plus delivery of a replacement spa.

What if my gate isn't wide enough?

We can sometimes tip spas on edge for narrow gates (48" minimum usually works). For very tight access, crane service is the solution. Send us photos for assessment.
