Frequently Asked Questions

How much does my safe or toolbox weigh?

Most residential gun safes range from 200-800 lbs empty. Professional toolboxes typically weigh 150-600 lbs when loaded. We can help estimate weight based on your model, or you can check the manufacturer's specifications. When in doubt, we bring equipment rated for heavier loads.

Will you damage my floors or walls?

Absolutely not. We use professional floor protection systems, moving blankets, and specialized equipment to prevent any damage to your property. Our technicians are trained to navigate tight spaces safely. If damage occurs (extremely rare), we're fully insured to cover repairs.

Can you move safes up or down stairs?

Yes! We handle stair moves regularly using hydraulic stair-climbing dollies and proper lifting techniques. Each flight of stairs adds $75 to the base price. We'll survey the route beforehand to ensure safe passage and may recommend door/railing removal for particularly heavy safes.

How far in advance do I need to schedule?

Most moves can be scheduled within 2-3 days. Same-day emergency service is available for a 25% premium. During busy seasons (summer moves), we recommend booking at least a week in advance. Call us - we'll work around your schedule whenever possible.

Do you service the entire Phoenix metro area?

Yes! We serve all of Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Gilbert, Chandler, Tempe, Glendale, Peoria, and surrounding areas within 25 miles at our base rates. We also service Tucson and other Arizona locations - distance charges apply for moves over 25 miles from central Phoenix.

What if my safe won't fit through the door?

We're experts at navigating tight spaces! Our team can remove door frames, railings, and even windows if needed (additional fees apply). We always survey the route during our quote process to identify potential challenges and plan solutions in advance.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We're fully licensed for commercial moving in Arizona and carry comprehensive insurance coverage for your property and our equipment. We're also part of the Curl Moving family of services, with years of professional moving experience.

Can you install (bolt down) my safe after moving it?

Yes! We offer bolt-down installation service for $75. This includes drilling into concrete, securing with appropriate bolts, and ensuring your safe meets security standards. We can also advise on optimal placement for both security and accessibility.
