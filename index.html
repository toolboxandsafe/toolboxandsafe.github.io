Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to help with the move?

Nope! I just need access to both pick up and drop off locations and I handle the rest. I bring all the equipment needed.

When do I pay?

You can pay after the service is complete. I accept Cash, Check, Venmo, Zelle, and Cash App.

Can you do same-day pickup and delivery?

Generally yes, but sometimes I have moves in-between especially for longer distance moves. I finalize the schedule the day before so you know exactly what will happen on move day.

Can you move my safe/toolbox up or down stairs?

Yes! I handle stair moves regularly. Each flight adds $75 to the price. Just let me know about any stairs, tight turns, or obstacles when you reach out for a quote.

What info do you need for a quote?

Text me the exact weight (or a picture so I can estimate), both addresses, and let me know if it's garage to garage or inside the house. Also mention any stairs, tight turns, non-paved routes, or obstacles.

Should I empty my toolbox before the move?

You can leave it loaded! Just lock the drawers and remove anything loose on the outside. If you have a top or side hutch, let me know if it's bolted - I'll run a strap around it either way.

What areas do you service?

I'm based in Gilbert and service all of Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Chandler, Tempe, and surrounding areas. I can go outside Phoenix metro too - just ask.

Can you bolt down my safe after moving it?

Yes! Bolt-down service is $75. I drill into concrete and secure it properly. Just let me know when you request a quote.
