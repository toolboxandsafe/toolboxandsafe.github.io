Our Pool Table Moving Process

1

Assessment & Quote

We'll ask about your table type, size, slate configuration, and both pickup and delivery locations. This helps us give you an accurate quote and come prepared with the right equipment.

2

Careful Disassembly

We remove and bag all hardware, carefully detach the rails, and lift each slate piece off the frame. Everything is labeled so reassembly goes smoothly.

3

Protected Transport

Slate pieces are individually padded and secured. Rails, legs, and frame components are protected and loaded carefully. Nothing shifts during transport.

4

Professional Reassembly

We reassemble the frame, place and align the slate, level everything precisely, and ensure your table plays true. We don't leave until you're satisfied.

7' tables are easier to handle than 9' tables. Three-piece slate requires more labor than single-piece. Antique tables need extra care.

Pool Table Moving FAQ

How much does it cost to move a pool table?

Pool table moving typically starts around $300-$500 for local moves, depending on table size, slate configuration, and whether disassembly/reassembly is needed. Three-piece slate tables cost more than single-piece due to the additional labor. Call for a free quote specific to your table.

Do pool tables need to be disassembled?

In most cases, yes. Professional slate-top pool tables should be disassembled for safe transport. The slate pieces are heavy (300-500 lbs each) and can crack if moved assembled. We handle complete disassembly and reassembly.

Can you move a pool table upstairs?

Yes! Since the table is disassembled, individual pieces can be safely carried up or down stairs. We have the equipment and experience for stair moves, though there may be an additional fee depending on the number of flights.

Will the table need to be re-leveled?

Yes, pool tables require precise leveling after any move. This is included in our service - we level your table and align the slate seams before we leave. Your table will be ready to play.

Do you move antique pool tables?

Absolutely. We have experience with antique Brunswick, Oliver Briggs, and other vintage tables. These require extra care due to their age, and we take additional precautions to protect these valuable pieces.

How long does a pool table move take?

Plan for 3-5 hours total including disassembly, transport, and reassembly. Larger tables, difficult access, or stairs can add time. We'll give you a time estimate when we provide your quote.
