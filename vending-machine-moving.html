What Makes Vending Machine Moving Different?

Commercial vending equipment presents unique challenges that regular movers aren't equipped to handle:

⚖️ Heavy & Top-Heavy

Vending machines typically weigh 600-900 lbs. The glass front and product trays make them top-heavy, requiring proper balance during transport.

❄️ Refrigeration Systems

Beverage and frozen machines have compressors that must be kept upright. Tilting can damage the refrigeration system - expensive repairs you don't want.

🔌 Electrical Components

Bill validators, credit card readers, and electronic components need protection from shocks and moisture during the move.

🚪 Access Challenges

Vending machines often go in tight break rooms, narrow hallways, or require elevator moves. We have the equipment and experience.

Our Vending Machine Moving Process

  1. Pre-Move Coordination: We'll discuss machine type, current location, destination, and any access challenges. You'll get an accurate quote upfront.
  2. Preparation: Empty products from the machine, unplug 24-48 hours before (for refrigerated units), and secure any loose parts.
  3. Professional Moving: We use commercial-grade equipment including appliance dollies, straps, and protective padding to safely transport your machine.
  4. Placement & Setup: We'll position the machine exactly where you need it. For refrigerated units, allow 24 hours before plugging in.

Vending Machine Moving FAQs

How much does it cost to move a vending machine?

Vending machine moving typically starts around $200-$300 for local moves within the Phoenix metro area. Final pricing depends on machine type (combo units are heavier), distance, floor level, and accessibility. Challenging moves and difficult access add to the cost. We provide free quotes for all vending machine moves.

Do you need to empty the vending machine before moving?

Yes, we strongly recommend emptying all product from the machine before we arrive. This reduces weight by 100-200 lbs, prevents product damage from shifting, and keeps the machine properly balanced during transport. For refrigerated machines, removing product also prevents spoilage if the move takes time.

Can you handle difficult vending machine access?

Yes, we have specialized equipment including heavy-duty dollies and proper rigging techniques to safely move vending machines between floors. There's typically an additional fee of $50-$100 depending on access difficulty and machine weight.

Do you move frozen and refrigerated vending machines?

Absolutely. We regularly move frozen and refrigerated vending machines. The key is proper preparation: turn the machine off 24-48 hours before the move to allow defrosting and draining. After the move, the machine should sit upright for at least 24 hours before being turned back on - this lets the compressor oil settle properly.

Are you insured to move commercial vending equipment?

Yes, we're fully insured for commercial equipment moving including vending machines. We carry liability insurance that protects both the equipment and property during the move. We can provide certificates of insurance for commercial clients who require them.

Do you do long-distance vending machine moves?

Yes, we handle vending machine moves throughout Arizona including Tucson, Flagstaff, and everywhere in between. For moves outside the Phoenix metro area, we calculate pricing based on distance and provide an upfront quote.
